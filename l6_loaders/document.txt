Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming how we interact with technology. 
From chatbots to recommendation systems, AI helps automate tasks and improve decision-making.

Machine learning, a subset of AI, allows systems to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. 
For example, a spam filter learns to identify unwanted emails based on patterns.

Large Language Models (LLMs) like Gemini and GPT can understand and generate human-like text. 
They are used in applications such as virtual assistants, content generation, and coding support.

However, AI also comes with challenges, including data privacy, bias, and ethical concerns. 
It is important to design AI systems responsibly to ensure fairness and transparency.

In the future, AI is expected to become even more powerful, impacting industries like healthcare, finance, and education.
